Traces of Motion
Description

Movement can carry meaning. Within this curious artifact dwells a tiny mind that understands motion. Every gesture leaves a trace across three silent axes. Most of these traces are meaningless noise. But some patterns repeat often enough that even a small machine can learn to recognize them. The message itself is not written in plain text. It is written in motion. Long before the device was built, someone left behind these lines… When thunder wakes the silent land, the earth must tremble in your hand. The restless world then starts to spin, a turning wheel from deep within. Bow to the west where shadows fall, then greet the east that wakes us all. Knock twice upon the ancient gate, for those who move may change their fate. When storms return and mountains shake, repeat the motion that you make. The makers of this curious device spent many seasons observing the movements of the world. From tremors of the earth to the turning of wheels, they gathered countless examples and taught the tiny mind within to recognize the rhythms of motion. Yet, as any good master knows, true understanding is not proven in the lesson hall. A few movements were never shown to the apprentice. They were kept aside for the day the artifact would leave its workshop.
